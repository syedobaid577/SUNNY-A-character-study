About Sunny

Sunny: The Silent Protagonist

Sunny is the main protagonist of OMORI, a reclusive and quiet boy who retreats into his own imagination following a traumatic event. He spends most of his time in his room, escaping reality through dreams and fantasies.

As the story progresses, players uncover Sunny's complex psychology and the events that shaped his current state. His journey is one of self-discovery, acceptance, and confronting painful truths.
