Lore & Timeline

Childhood

Sunny spends his early years happily with Mari and his family before any major incidents.

The Incident

Sunny accidentally causes Mari's death, which becomes the source of his trauma and withdrawal.

Isolation

Sunny retreats to his room and creates White Space to escape reality and repress his guilt.

Reconnecting

He slowly reconnects with friends Kel, Aubrey, and Hero, beginning to face the past he has hidden from everyone.

Confrontation

Sunny finally confronts the truth about Mari's death and must choose between denial and acceptance.

Healing

Sunny comes to terms with his trauma, faces his guilt, and begins the journey of self-forgiveness.
